Our Heart

Why We Do This

♡

There is a reason this space exists.

Not from theory.

Not from observation.

But from lived experience.

It begins with a story.
And healing is not only personal…
it is also shared.

01✿

YOUR STORY

There was a season in my life where I found myself drowning in guilt and shame—embarrassed by the position I was in. I never imagined my life would look that way.

The weight of it felt heavy. Pride and shame held me in a chokehold.

I couldn't bring myself to share what I was going through—not with family, not with anyone.

So when it became too much—and it often did— I would cry.

It helped for a moment…
but it wasn't healing.

02✿

THE SHIFT

One day, something shifted.

It was pressed upon my heart that our Father already knew everything about me—what I had done, what I was doing, and what I would do—and still desired a relationship with me.

I didn't understand it at first.
But over time… I allowed myself to receive it.

And something in me began to change— not all at once, but deeply.

He knew me
and still
wanted me.

♡

03✿

WHERE HEALING BEGAN

Healing didn't start with having the right words or having everything figured out.

It started with honesty.

I wasn't ready to face everything.
So I didn't.
I started with one area—the one affecting me the most.

And when I was finally honest…
that's where everything started to shift.

“

It started
with honesty.

“
04✿

TURNING POINT

Everything began to change.

I stopped running and brought that brokenness before Him.

This was the beginning of a different way of living.

What I encountered… I didn't expect.

Yahweh met me
in the middle of my
brokenness.

♡

Not when I had it together.
Not when I had answers.
But right there—in the mess.

And He didn't turn away.
Instead, He embraced me with a love I had never felt before.

Compassion. Love.

He restored me—
piece by piece,
truth by truth,
love by love.

And then came a step
I didn't want to take:
“Share your story.”

I resisted.
But not long after, a friend called me—sharing her struggles.
Her story sounded just like mine.
So I shared what I had walked through.

Weeks later, she asked me,
“How did you know my story?”

I didn't.
I just knew mine.

And in that moment,
I realized something:

We are not as
alone as we think
we are.

❖

And that truth became
the foundation of this ministry.

05✿

WHY THIS EXISTS

There Is H.O.P.E. Ministries was born from that place—a place of Healing. Overcoming. Purpose. Empowerment.

Because too many people are silently hurting.
Too many are carrying what they were never meant to carry.
Too many feel like they have to do it alone.

And healing needs a path—not just a moment.

06♡

WHAT WE
BELIEVE

Healing is
possible.

Wholeness
is real.

Your story
is not over.

What has been buried can be uncovered, healed, and renewed.
Identity can be restored.
Peace can return.
Purpose can be rediscovered.

07✿

CLOSING

We're not here to fix you
or turn you into something else.

We're here to walk with you—

as you heal,
as you rediscover who you are,
and as you step into the life
you were meant to live.

And that
journey has
a rhythm.
